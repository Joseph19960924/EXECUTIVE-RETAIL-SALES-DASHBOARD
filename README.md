# 📊 Executive Retail Sales Dashboard

## Overview

The **Executive Retail Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI** to analyze retail sales performance and support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard transforms transactional retail data into meaningful insights through executive KPIs, interactive visualizations, and dynamic filtering.

This project demonstrates the ability to analyze business performance, identify revenue drivers, evaluate customer behavior, and communicate insights through a professional dashboard suitable for executive stakeholders.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transaction data every day. Without an effective reporting solution, decision-makers may struggle to monitor performance, identify trends, and respond quickly to changes in customer demand.

This dashboard was developed to provide a centralized view of retail performance, enabling stakeholders to monitor key business metrics and make informed decisions.

---

## Business Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze sales trends over time.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Compare sales across different sales channels.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate regional performance.
* Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting.

---

## Business Questions Answered

### 1. How is the business performing overall?

The dashboard provides executive KPIs including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Transactions
* Average Order Value
* Units Sold

---

### 2. How are sales changing over time?

A time-series visualization tracks revenue over the reporting period, allowing users to identify:

* Growth trends
* Seasonal patterns
* Sales fluctuations

---

### 3. What is driving revenue?

Product category and sales channel visualizations highlight the primary contributors to overall revenue.

---

### 4. Who are the most valuable customers?

Customer segment analysis compares purchasing behavior across:

* New Customers
* Returning Customers
* Loyal Customers
* VIP Customers

---

### 5. Where are sales strongest?

Regional analysis compares sales performance across different geographic regions to identify opportunities and areas requiring attention.
